While exercising, there is no "one fits all" approach. Every individual reacts differently to various forms of feedback, highlighting the importance of tailoring exercise programs to meet each person's unique needs and preferences. This project aims to explore and identify personalized feedback methods that will best support and enhance an individual's fitness journey. 

This work is being done under the supervision of [Dr. Aaron Steinfeld](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~astein/), in collaboration with [Roshni Kaushik](https://roshk99.github.io/index.html) and [Dr. Reid Simmons](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~reids/), at Carnegie Mellon University.

All work is funded through the [AI-CARING](https://ai-caring.org/) project.
